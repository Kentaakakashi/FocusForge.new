Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You'll need to choose a unique username, a display name, and a password. No email is required!

How does the Pomodoro timer work?

The Pomodoro technique involves studying for 25 minutes, then taking a 5-minute break. After 4 sessions, take a longer 15-30 minute break. You can customize these times in the settings.

Can I use StudyZen on multiple devices?

Yes! Your data is stored locally in your browser. To sync across devices, make sure you're using the same browser and have enabled sync in your browser settings.

How do I reset my password?

Since StudyZen doesn't use email verification, you'll need to remember your password. We recommend using a password manager. If you forget your password, you'll need to create a new account.

What happens to my data if I clear browser cache?

StudyZen stores data in your browser's localStorage. If you clear your browser cache or use incognito mode, your data will be lost. We recommend regularly exporting your data from the Statistics page.
